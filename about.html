ABOUT US

WHAT IS NEXT?

NEXT is an initiative from the Faculty of Media and Creative Arts at Humber College, formerly known as The Adcentre, that has been running since 2016.

YOU ARE IT, YOU ARE NEXT.

NEXT was originally intended to refer to our next generation and represent legacy. Founder X is a Humber student who graduated 10 years ago and is now part of Faculty of Media and Creative Arts at Humber College, which in turn gives NEXT a different meaning.

WHAT WE DO?

We cooperate with external organizations that have a creative challenge or project in the fields of marketing, advertising, publics relations, digital communications, and or design, to work with our students.

OUR HISTORY

This year marks the eighth year of NEXT's existence, and in an era where science and technology are on the rise and our lives have changed drastically, Generation Z is the generation of innovation. They are growing up in the midst of change, daring to challenge and take risks, and as NEXT embraces change, we hope to put the learning, ideas, and insights of our Humber students into practice. This is a new milestone, building bridges between students and social enterprises through an open and transparent online platform, creating more opportunities for everyone and practicing fairness and sustainability in employment.
